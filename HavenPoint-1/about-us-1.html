Setting the Standard in Luxury Real Estate

Founded in 2002, our company has grown from a boutique agency with a handful of agents to one of the most respected names in luxury real estate. With a commitment to exceptional service and unparalleled expertise, we've helped thousands of clients find their perfect property and achieve their real estate investment goals.

Our Journey Through the Years

2002

Foundation

Established with just 5 agents in a small office in Beverly Hills, focusing exclusively on high-end residential properties.

Expansion

Opened our second office in Manhattan, NY, and expanded our team to 25 agents, establishing our presence on both coasts.

2007
2012

Digital Innovation

Launched our first digital platform, revolutionizing how clients search for and experience luxury properties with virtual tours.

International Reach

Established our first international partnerships, extending our services to luxury markets in London, Paris, and Dubai.

2016
2022

Headquarters Unveiling

Opened our new global headquarters, a state-of-the-art facility designed to reflect our commitment to excellence and sustainability.
