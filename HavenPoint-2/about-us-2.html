Setting the Standard in Luxury Real Estate

Founded in 2002, our company has grown from a boutique agency with three agents to one of the most respected names in luxury real estate. With headquarters in Beverly Hills and offices across major metropolitan areas, we specialize in connecting discerning clients with exceptional properties that reflect their lifestyle and investment goals.

Our team of over 150 dedicated professionals combines deep market knowledge with personalized service to create an unparalleled real estate experience. Whether you're seeking a waterfront estate, urban penthouse, or investment opportunity, our commitment to excellence ensures that every client interaction exceeds expectations.

Jonathan Blackwell

Founder & CEO

Our Journey

From our humble beginnings to becoming an industry leader, explore the key milestones that have shaped our company's growth and success over the years.

2002

Company Founded

Jonathan Blackwell establishes the company with a vision to transform the luxury real estate experience, opening our first office in Beverly Hills with just three agents.

2007

Expansion to New Markets

After five successful years, we expand operations to New York and Miami, bringing our distinctive approach to luxury real estate to new markets on both coasts.

2012

Technology Innovation

We launch our proprietary virtual property tour platform, revolutionizing how luxury properties are showcased online and setting a new industry standard for digital marketing.

2017

Global Partnerships

We establish strategic partnerships with elite real estate firms in Europe and Asia, creating a global network that allows us to serve clients with international property interests.

2022

20th Anniversary & New Headquarters

We celebrate 20 years of excellence by opening our new state-of-the-art headquarters in Beverly Hills, designed to reflect our commitment to sustainability and cutting-edge client service.

2025

Present Day & Future Vision

Today, with 12 offices nationwide and a team of over 150 professionals, we continue to innovate and expand our services while maintaining our core commitment to exceptional client experiences.

Corporate Social Responsibility

We're committed to making a positive impact in the communities where we operate through sustainable practices and charitable initiatives.

Community Housing Initiative

Our flagship program partners with local organizations to build and renovate affordable housing in underserved communities. Since 2010, we've contributed over $3.5 million and 5,000 volunteer hours to create safe, quality homes for more than 200 families.

  • Annual build events in all 12 of our office locations
  • Employee volunteer program with paid time off for community service
  • Matching donation program for employee contributions

Environmental Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint and promoting sustainable real estate practices. Our headquarters achieved LEED Platinum certification in 2022, and we're implementing green initiatives across all our offices.

  • Carbon-neutral operations through offsets and renewable energy
  • Specialized expertise in marketing and selling sustainable luxury properties
  • Educational programs for clients on eco-friendly home features
